Why Italy?

You can find everything you are looking for and even more

Italy, a country in south-central Europe, occupies a peninsula that juts deep into the Mediterranean Sea. Italy comprises some of the most varied and scenic landscapes on Earth and is often described as a country shaped like a boot. At its broad top stand the Alps, which are among the world’s most rugged mountains. Italy’s highest points are along Monte Rosa, which peaks in Switzerland, and along Mont Blanc, which peaks in France. The western Alps overlook a landscape of Alpine lakes and glacier-carved valleys that stretch down to the Po River and Piedmont. Tuscany, to the south of the Cisalpine region, is perhaps the country’s best-known region.

Arguably Europe’s most enticing country, Italy charms visitors with irresistible food, awesome architecture, diverse scenery and unparalleled art. In fact, it's so packed with possibilities that it can almost overwhelm. Everyone knows Italy – Rome’s iconic ruins, Tuscany’s vine-clad hills, and Venice’s romantic waterways. These have all been on traveler’s radars for centuries. Add fabulous food and breathtaking scenery, and you have all the ingredients for an unforgettable visit. Italy has 20 regions, each with its own unique blend of natural beauty, culinary delights, and cultural treasures. You could choose what to explore depending on your preferences.

Il Guscio has been around since 1986 and has a menu that takes homestyle Tuscan and Italian classics to gourmet status.

Address:

Via dell'Orto, 49, 50124 Firenze, Florence
